Rice and Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Required salt
  • Cooking oil
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 2 cloves leaf

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the rice grains are clean.
  2. In a large pot, combine the rinsed rice, water, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, cloves, and salt.
  3. Boil the water and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed.
  4. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add mustard seed.When they start to splutter, add curry leaves, cinnamon stick, and cardamom pods.
  5. Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent.
  6. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  7. Add the chicken or vegetables to the pan, and cook until browned.
  8. Stir in turmeric powder, curry powder, and chili powder. Mix well to coat the chicken or vegetables evenly.
  9. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the mixture is well combined.
  10. Pour in the coconut milk, season with salt.
  11. Cover the pan and let it simmer for about 20-30 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  12. Adjust the seasoning to taste.
