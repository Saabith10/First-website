Recipe Catalog

01.Rice And Curry

Savor the taste of Sri Lanka with our signature Rice and Curry dish. From spicy chicken curry to comforting dhal, our dish encapsulates the island's culinary heritage, delivering a delightful blend of spices and textures.

02.Milk Rice(Kiribath)

Sri Lankan Milk Rice, known locally as 'Kiribath' is simple yet exquisite dish that captures the heart of the island's culinary heritage, offering a taste of comfort and festivity in every spoonful."

03.Cheese Koththu

Cheese Koththu, a delectable variation of the popular Sri Lankan street food, Koththu Roti offers a unique and savory twist to traditional flavors. What sets Cheese Koththu apart is the addition of creamy and melted cheese, infusing a rich and indulgent texture into the medley of ingredients.

04.Fried Rice

Served as a side dish or a main course, fried rice is a comfort food favorite enjoyed worldwide for its simplicity, versatility, and mouthwatering appeal.
