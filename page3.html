Milk Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup grain rice
  • 2 cups thick coconut milk
  • 2 and a half cups of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Jaggery or sugar(optional)

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a large pot, combine the rice, water, and salt.
  3. Boil the rice until the rice is cooked and has absorbed most of the water.
  4. Once the rice is almost cooked, add sufficient amount of thick coconut milk to the pot.
  5. Stir well and continue to simmer until the rice is fully cooked and has absorbed the coconut milk.
  6. Taste the rice and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Add more salt if needed.
  7. Cut the rice into a desired shape and serve
